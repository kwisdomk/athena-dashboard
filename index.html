Priority Queue

RAM Compatibility

Must be DDR4-3200 SO-DIMM. Do not buy DDR4-2666 or DDR5. Second slot must match exactly for dual-channel.

SSD Slot

The M.2 slot is PCIe Gen 4x4 but a Gen 3 drive (Dahua) will work at Gen 3 speeds. Don't overpay for Gen 4.

Benchmark Baseline

Run Cinebench R23 and CrystalDiskMark before any changes. Document before/after for Scylla records.

Battery Care

Enable 80% charge cap in HP Command Center immediately. Free, high impact on cell longevity.
